I wanted to put a tip on how I usually remove my stickers because it can be tough!


Surfaces like laptops and or tablets can leave the sticker to have residue when removed.

I usually use 90% rubbing alcohol with a cloth towel to clean the residue.

The adhesion will properly be easy to remove after that!

Do not put too much alcohol and only clean with the cloth slightly damp.
