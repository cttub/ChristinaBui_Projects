Glossy stickers are made with matte stickers but have a laminate overlay to give more protection and glossy shine to the sticker.

This ensures a better quality of my homemade stickers with the resources I have.


To ensure proper use of these stickers, it is recommended to peel from the back of the sticker so the laminate does not tear off the sticker.

I usually try to provide indicators of areas that are easy to peel.


These stickers are not recommended to bend in any way.

This means the use of putting these stickers on round surfaces, like water bottles, does not ensure proper adhesion.
